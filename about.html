About Zenterra

Connecting global industries through reliable commodity trading and innovative logistics solutions

Who We Are

Zenterra is an international commodity trading company serving clients across Asia, the Gulf, Europe, and Africa. We specialize in raw materials for steel production and are expanding into grain commodities and integrated logistics.

Our company was founded on the principles of reliability, transparency, and innovation in global commodity trading. We understand the critical importance of consistent supply chains in today's interconnected world, and we've built our business to deliver exactly that.

With deep industry expertise and strategic partnerships across multiple continents, Zenterra has established itself as a trusted intermediary in the complex world of international commodity trading.
