Steelmaking Raw Materials

Our core expertise lies in providing essential raw materials for steel production. We source and supply high-quality materials that meet international standards and specifications.

Iron Ore Concentrate

High-grade iron ore concentrate with consistent iron content, optimized for blast furnace operations and steel production efficiency.

Iron Ore Pellets

Premium quality iron ore pellets designed for direct reduction processes and blast furnace charging with superior physical properties.

DRI / HBI

Direct Reduced Iron (DRI) and Hot Briquetted Iron (HBI) for electric arc furnace operations and specialized steel production.

Pig Iron

High-quality pig iron for foundries and steel mills, available in various grades to meet specific production requirements.

Billets & Slabs

Semi-finished steel products including square billets and steel slabs for downstream processing and manufacturing.

Rebars, CRC, HRC

Finished steel products including reinforcement bars, cold-rolled coils, and hot-rolled coils for construction and manufacturing.
